## Key Principles Applied

1. **Specificity Over Abstraction**
   - Before: "decentralized exchange of ideas"
   - After: "Workshops in Shoreditch. Demo day in King's Cross."

2. **Data Over Claims**
   - Before: "London's tech scene is distributed"
   - After: "321 companies, 50+ workspaces, 10 square miles"

3. **Short Sentences**
   - Before: "Traditional tech conferences put everyone in a dark room to listen to a single broadcast."
   - After: "Most conferences centralize." [new paragraph] "We do the opposite."

4. **Parallel Structure**
   - "We don't run events. We make them discoverable."
   - "We don't build venues. We activate existing nodes."
   - "We don't consolidate. We coordinate."

5. **Remove Jargon Where It Doesn't Add Value**
   - Before: "Sync your calendar with nodes that match your frequency"
   - After: "Build your path through the city"

6. **Keep Technical Language Where It Defines Position**
   - Kept: "protocol layer," "nodes," "network"
   - Removed: "bandwidth," "comm-link," "frequency"
