# Emergence London 2026

**London's tech scene, emerging.**

Three days. Fifty venues. One city finally seeing itself.

🌐 **Live:** https://emergence-landing.vercel.app

---

## What is Emergence?

Distributed tech conference across London, April 28-30, 2026. SXSW-style model where offices, cafes, warehouses, and studios become active nodes in the network.

**Not a conference. A peer-to-peer protocol for London's tech ecosystem to see itself.**

---

## How It Works

- **Host a Node:** Turn your space into an event venue
- **Build Your Path:** Navigate between micro-summits, demos, debates
- **Power the Grid:** Sponsors inject capital directly into nodes

**Central Hub:** Kachette (Shoreditch, EC1V 9LP)
